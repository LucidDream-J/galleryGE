At Gallery East:

We represent emerging and established contemporary Western Australian artists.

specialise in the traditional and contemporary arts and crafts of Japan and those Australian and international artists who have been influenced by Japanese aesthetics.

We are members of the Art Consulting Association of Australia; the Society for Japanese Arts; and the Asian Arts Society of Australia.
